    About this extension

    I created this extension for myself, to quickly log off from social media sites when I need to focus on something.

    The extension closes the sessions from the list of user-specified websites by deleting their cookies.

    Source code: github.com/diegolas-code/one-click-logout

    Based on the extension Remove Cookies For Site by Nestor Urquiza github.com/nestoru
